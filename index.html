¿So who is this guy?

My name is Felipe Torres. I'm a software engineer by choice, an educator at heart . I'm very passionate about software communities. JS and NodeJS evangelist. A Chilean living in Santiago de Chile.

More than anything I enjoy solving problems using technology, I'm the kind of person that gets bored without a challenge. I currently work at Groupon (Just in case you didn't notice), which it has a lot of interesting problems to solve.

I like working with interesting people, learning, teaching and being able to communicate that, that's why I run the JSMeetups in Santiago, do the NodeSchool Santiago events , and why I started Noders .

On my free time I co-host a podcast ( Itunes , Stitcher ) , I'm taking salsa lessons and hike whenever I can, sometimes I post on twitter. I've worked at various startups and with very interesting people, you can read more on my linkedin profile or by looking onto my cv.

¿Got any questions? Maybe some feedback, or just want to say hi, (That would be awesome, I'm always up for meeting cool people) you can shoot me an email: felipe.torressepulveda@gmail.com.
